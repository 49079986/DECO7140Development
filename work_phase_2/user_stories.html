User Stories

The Following Is an Oral Account of the Interviewer's Experience

Jake Thompson-- A 19-years-old Student in College:

I'm Jake Thompson, a 19-year-old economics student. I'm usually busy with my studies, but when it comes to cooking, I seek speed and simplicity. I often visit food websites to look for quick recipes on Kuaishou, preferably those that can be prepared with just a microwave oven or an electric rice cooker. Sometimes I have no idea what to do at all and just hope that the website can recommend recipes based on what ingredients I have in my fridge.

I like refreshing and interesting website designs. If there are too many advertisements or the steps are unclear, I will directly close them. It would be even better if there were pictures, short videos and others' comments on the recipes.

RESPONSE:

As a busy college student, I want to quickly find simple recipes based on the ingredients I have, so that I can cook fast meals without stress or wasted time.

Linda Zhang - A 40-years-old Housewife:

I'm Linda Zhang. I'm 40 years old and I'm the mother of two children. Cooking is my daily compulsory course, so I often search online for nutritious dishes suitable for my children. A good website is most importantly characterized by clear practices, easily accessible materials, and preferably video guidance.

I also want to share my signature dishes, but I hope the uploading steps won't be too complicated. I like warm and tidy designs. The most important thing is that they can be clearly seen on the mobile phone.

RESPONSE

As a stay-at-home mom, I want clear, detailed, and kid-friendly recipes with optional video guidance, so that I can prepare healthy meals for my family with confidence.

David Harris - A 28-years-old Product Manager:

I'm David Harris. I'm 28 years old and I work as a product manager in an Internet company. I'm usually busy with work. I cook by myself mainly for health and to save money on takeout. For me, the most important thing for a food website is efficiency: straightforward steps, the ability to filter time and difficulty, and a friendly mobile interface.

I like websites with a minimalist style. It would be very practical if they could give recommendations based on the ingredients I have or have a user rating system.

RESPONSE

As a single office worker, I want an efficient, mobile-friendly site that recommends meals based on available ingredients and prep time, so that I can eat well despite my busy schedule.
