User Stories

The Following Is an Oral Account of the Interviewer's Experience

Jake Thompson-- A 19-years-old Student in College:

Hi, I’m Jake. I’m a 19-year-old college student majoring in media and communications. I’ve been into gaming ever since I was a kid. From Super Mario to Fortnite, I’ve always enjoyed playing and competing. Lately, I’ve been focusing on team-based multiplayer games like Valorant and League of Legends, where you need a good team and strategy to win. But finding teammates who are active and match my skill level is a bit of a challenge. I often end up playing with random people, which is fine, but it’s hard to build consistent teams for ranked matches.

A couple of weeks ago, I joined an online tournament with some random players, and let’s just say, it didn’t go well. We didn’t communicate properly, and some of the players weren’t really trying. We got knocked out early, and it felt like a wasted opportunity. That experience made me realize how important it is to find teammates who are not just skilled but also serious about the game. That’s why I need a website that helps me find like-minded players and keep up with all the latest esports events.

RESPONSE:

As a passionate gamer, I want to have an interactive community with game discussions, team-finding, and esports updates so that I can stay connected with other players, improve my skills, and never miss out on exciting gaming events.

Ethan Miller - A 29-years-old Programmer:

Hey, I’m Ethan. I’m 29 and work as a software developer. Most of my day is spent coding and debugging, so by the time I get home, I want to unwind with a good game. I’m into strategy games and multiplayer shooters. Civilization VI is one of my favorites for long gaming sessions, but I also enjoy Apex Legends when I need something faster-paced.

One thing I’ve noticed recently is that I don’t have the time I used to have to browse through all kinds of gaming sites. I used to enjoy reading reviews and diving into discussions about upcoming releases, but now I just want something quick and to the point. The other day, I missed out on a huge Steam sale because I wasn’t actively tracking the games I wanted. That’s when I realized how much more efficient a website could be for me—something that not only tracks my favorite games but also keeps me updated on deals and discounts.

RESPONSE

As a busy professional, I want a streamlined and efficient platform with personalized game recommendations, discount tracking, and performance analytics so that I can make the most of my limited gaming time and stay competitive without unnecessary distractions.

David Harris - A 39-years-old Player:

Hello, I’m David. I’m 39, married, and a father of two. I work in sales, and after a busy day, I love unwinding with a game. I’ve been gaming since I was a kid, from Super Nintendo to PlayStation. These days, I prefer casual, relaxing games like Stardew Valley or Slay the Spire. I don’t need anything too competitive—I just want something enjoyable to pass the time when I have a few free hours.

Recently, I spent hours trying to find a new game to try. I went through multiple gaming sites, but most of them were geared towards hardcore players or had endless lists of games that felt overwhelming. I ended up picking a game, but it didn’t turn out to be what I expected. The whole process was frustrating. I realized that I need a website that’s more straightforward—one that curates games based on my preferences without all the noise. Something simple, clear, and easy to use.

RESPONSE

As a casual gamer, I want a simple and user-friendly website with curated game recommendations, beginner-friendly guides, and minimal social distractions so that I can easily find enjoyable games that fit my lifestyle without feeling overwhelmed.
