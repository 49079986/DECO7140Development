Ethics Analysis

Problems May Raise:

  • Account abuse/data tracking
    • If the website requires registration, there are concerns that mobile phone numbers and email addresses may be leaked or used for advertising push, as well as that personal browsing and collecting habits may be overly recorded or used for advertising activities.
  • False or misleading recipes
    • It's easy to make mistakes. For instance, if you follow along and it turns out to be a complete failure, especially since she doesn't have much cooking experience.
  • Too many advertisements cause frequent operations
    • If a page is too complicated or has too many advertisements, it will make people feel troublesome or reduce their sense of trust.
  • The content uploaded/obtained is not protected by copyright
    • If you upload your own original family recipes, you may worry that the content will be plagiarized or not signed, which would cause your efforts to be in vain.
  • The lack of hierarchical design leads to troublesome use
    • Some people think it's difficult, while others think it's simple. Without a good layered design, people will lose interest in exploration.

Way to Solve These Problems:

  • Privacy and data security
    • Provide clear and transparent data usage instructions (privacy policy is easy to understand)
    • Users can choose whether to enable positioning, personalized recommendation and other functions
    • Data encryption and minimum collection principles (collect only necessary information)
    • Provide the "Visitor Browsing" function and do not force login
  • The recipe is not true.
    • Introduce a recipe review mechanism, conduct preliminary detection on the content uploaded by users, implement a user scoring mechanism, give priority to pushing high-scoring content, and add a comment section below the recipes
    • Add tags such as allergens and unsuitable groups to enable users to avoid them
  • Poor page experience
    • During the initial operation stage, it is recommended to use "no advertisements" as a selling point to enhance word-of-mouth. If there are advertisements in the later stage, keep the layout clean and clearly mark the word "promotion"
    • The search bar is prominent, the content classification is clear, the page loads quickly, and duplicate animations and useless jumps are avoided
    • All functions and contents operate smoothly on the mobile phone.
  • Original content is plagiarized/has no ownership
    • All user-uploaded content retains the right of authorship by default. The website is not allowed to use user content for commercial purposes without authorization. If it is used for recommendation pages, the author must be indicated
    • Provide an entry for "infringement reporting", establish a customer service handling process, and respond to content disputes
  • The functions do not fit the different levels of users
    • Beginner's Zone: such as "For Zero Cooking Skills", "Only Microwave Oven", Advanced zone: Special cuisine, slow stew, festival feasts, etc
    • Recommend content based on the preferences selected by the user (such as cooking experience, time limit), and optimize the recommendation accuracy based on the browsing history (this function can be optionally turned off).
    • Users can "follow" certain types of dishes (such as Japanese bento, low-calorie desserts), and the system will automatically push relevant content
