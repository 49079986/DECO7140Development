Ethics Analysis

Problems May Raise:

  • Data privacy and personalized recommendations:
    • These features often require the collection of a lot of personal data (gaming preferences, online time, skill level, etc.). Without a transparent privacy policy, the information could be used for purposes he did not consent to.
  • Adolescent addiction mechanism:
    • Young users are more likely to be affected by the "achievement system", "ranking", "stimulating feedback" and other mechanisms, if the website design is too addicted to the orientation, it may affect his study and life.
  • Community atmosphere and cyber violence:
    • Competitive games are often accompanied by arguments and verbal attacks, and if the site is not well policed by the community, Jake may encounter negative emotions and even personal attacks during interactions.
  • Algorithm bias and recommendation misdirection:
    • Hoping the system to recommend suitable games or track offers, but the algorithmic recommendations may be biased too much towards business interests rather than genuine interests, leading him to spend money on unsuitable games.

Way to Solve These Problems:

  • Privacy and data security
    • Provide clear and transparent data usage instructions (privacy policy is easy to understand)
    • Users can choose whether to enable positioning, personalized recommendation and other functions
    • Data encryption and minimum collection principles (collect only necessary information)
  • Game addiction/time management
    • Set up a "health alert" mechanism, such as reminding rest and limiting daily play time
    • Provide time tracking reports to let users understand their usage habits
    • Introduce "Focus Mode" or "No Distractions Mode"
  • Community atmosphere and cyber violence
    • Establish both automatic and manual content review mechanisms
    • Reporting system + transparent handling mechanism after reporting
    • Clear community guidelines, set penalties for users who violate the rules (ban, block)
  • Bias and transparency of algorithm recommendation
    • Allow users to view and control the parameters that influence recommendations (e.g. game type, price)
    • Label "promotional content" and "advertising content" to avoid confusing recommendations with advertising
    • Provide "reset recommendations" or "reverse feedback" mechanisms (e.g., don't like the game)
