About Us

We are a non-profit organisation dedicated to fostering social cohesion by empowering diverse communities to share their stories, connect with others, and access resources that enhance their visibility and influence. Our mission is to strengthen societal bonds by creating platforms that celebrate and support the unique identities of communities across Australia.

Project Overview

We are commissioning the development of dynamic, user-friendly websites that represent and support different communities. Each website should serve as a central hub where community members can share resources, highlight events, and foster meaningful connections. The websites must not include any form of commercial activity, such as advertising or selling products. However, it is acceptable to promote resources, events, or services that are directly beneficial to the community.

The websites must be accessible, mobile-friendly, and reflect the diverse needs of the selected community focus area while adhering to our mission of inclusivity, accessibility, and ethical design. Each website should also celebrate the unique characteristics of its chosen community and support meaningful interaction and engagement.

Community Focus Areas

  • Cultural and Heritage Communities
  • Academic and Professional Communities
  • Wellness and Mental Health Communities
  • Social and Environmental Advocacy Communities
  • Regional or International Communities
  • Creative Arts and Media Communities
  • Lived Experience Communities
  • Food and Culinary Communities
  • Tech and Innovation Communities

Additional Information

Target Audience

Each website must serve two key groups:

  1. Community Members: Individuals within the selected community who seek representation, resources, and connection.
  2. General Audience: Broader society, who can use the website to learn about the community and its contributions.

Accessibility must be prioritised to ensure seamless interaction for all users, including those relying on assistive technologies.

Project Goals

  1. Represent and celebrate the identity of the chosen community, recognising and representing the diversity within the community rather than relying on stereotypes.
  2. Provide clear, accessible information and resources to users.
  3. Foster meaningful connections and support within the community.
  4. Ensure ethical, inclusive, and culturally sensitive design principles are upheld.

Values - Needs - Feature Analysis

Values -- Why Do We Establish This Website?

  1. We Would Like to Solve These Problems:
    1. Let you have a richer and more balanced diet
    2. Save your cooking time
    3. Reduce food waste
    4. Enable you to consult recipes anytime and anywhere
    5. Recommend different delicacies to make your life more wonderful
    6. Share your joy and sense of achievement when cooking
    7. Make your meal plan

Needs -- Common Requirements

  • Simple and clear recipe steps:
    • Everyone hopes that the dishes can be written clearly, specifically and easily, avoiding vague expressions
  • Recommend recipes based on ingredients:
    • It is hoped that by inputting the existing materials, one can obtain the food that can be made and reduce the difficulty of choosing "what to eat today".
  • The website interface is simple and easy to use:
    • Don't like pages with too many advertisements or slow loading. It is hoped that the interface is clean and tidy and easy to browse on mobile phones.
  • Reliable content and community interaction:
    • Hoping there will be comments, ratings and experience sharing so that I can get references or inspirations from other users.

Needs -- Individual Needs

  1. Jake Thompson's Requirements
    • Quick and easy quick recipes
    • A young and interesting visual style
    • Beginner-friendly graphic or video tutorials
  2. Linda Zhang's Requirements
    • Nutritious recipes suitable for children and families
    • The recipe contains nutritional information and alternative suggestions
    • Support uploading and sharing of family signature dishes
    • Prefer a warm and tidy design style
  3. David Harris's Requirements
    • Efficient search function (by ingredients, time consumption, difficulty)
    • Mobile optimization is given priority and is suitable for browsing during fragmented time
    • Like minimalist style design and rating system

Features -- What We Need to Do?

  1. Homepage
    • Search box(You can search by ingredients, dish names, time, and difficulty)
    • Intelligent recommendation area(Push based on user preferences or popular tags)
    • Kuaishou student Meals | Family Nutritious Meals | After-work Quick Food Recommendations
    • Today's recommended recipe(Carousel or card display)
    • User Review List(Popular recipes with high ratings and numerous comments)
    • Entry navigation: Enter different pages by category (for example: "Cook in 5 Minutes", "Kids Love to Eat", "Fat Loss Bento")
  2. Recipes
      Clear classification:
    • By population: Students/Families/office workers
    • By ingredients: meat/Vegetables/main courses/snacks
    • By time: within 10 minutes/half an hour / 1 hour +
    • By function: fat-reducing meal/muscle-building meal/bento/Vegetarian
  3. Intelligent recommendation function
    • Users input existing ingredients(The system recommends recipes that can be made)
    • Optional filtration conditions: time, difficulty, whether it is vegetarian, whether it is free of allergens, etc
  4. Upload and share (user recipe)
    • Simple upload process: title + ingredients + steps + pictures
    • Review mechanism (to avoid spam information)
    • Users can receive likes and comments
    • It can be classified by tags such as "Family Dishes" and "Student Works"
  5. Community Interaction
    • Recipe comment section
    • Tips/Sharing of pitfall experiences
    • Text and image feedback of "I made this dish"
