Ethical Reasoning Report for the EasyPlate

Your Everyday Cooking Companion

I want to create a food sharing and recommendation platform suitable for different user groups, with a particular focus on the usage experience of students, office workers and family users.

  • Users: Students, office workers, housewives (such as Jake, David, Linda)
  • Client/Developer: We hope to create a brand website that users love
  • Community: A broader user group that may participate in content sharing, commenting, and interaction
  • Potential funders or partners: food brands, health institutions, content platforms
  • Privacy: Whether users disclose personal information when uploading content, commenting or saving recipes
  • Content credibility and source: Prevent the dissemination of unhealthy or false recipe information
  • Inclusiveness: Whether the website supports users from different backgrounds (culture, language, device, physical ability, etc.)
  • Consent mechanism: Whether the consent of users is explicitly obtained when sharing content and using the recommendation system

I will conduct an in-depth analysis of the ethical issues that may be encountered during the development and operation of this food-sharing platform

  • Public interest: Ensure that the website helps users have a healthy diet and does not mislead
  • Honesty and Trust: Transparent data usage and recommendation mechanisms
  • Fairness: Accessibility features ensure equal access for all users
  • Privacy: When users upload content or use functions, their data is respected and protected
  • Users lose trust in the platform and are reluctant to share content or use its functions
  • Misleading recommendations cause health problems (such as recipes that are not suitable for children)
  • Vulnerable users (such as those with visual impairments) are unable to use it due to the lack of assistive functions
  • Option: Mandatory Cookies and Privacy Statement

A letter for my clients:

Dear customer,

This can enhance user trust and expand the user base, further supporting your brand promotion and website influence.
