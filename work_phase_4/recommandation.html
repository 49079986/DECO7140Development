Chicken leg baby Chinese cabbage

The soup is delicious, the meat is tender and juicy, and the baby cabbage is sweet and fresh!

Recipe
×

Chicken leg baby Chinese cabbage

  • Remove the bones from the chicken legs and cut them into small cubes. Add a spoonful of cooking wine and a little black pepper
  • Mix well and marinate for 15 minutes
  • Heat oil and stir-fry minced garlic until fragrant
  • Add the diced chicken legs and stir-fry until cooked
  • Add the baby cabbage and stir-fry until soft
  • Add one spoonful of light soy sauce, oyster sauce and a little salt, stir-fry evenly, and sprinkle with chopped scallions

Chicken breast with button mushrooms

The chicken is crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. The button mushrooms are tender and juicy. It has a black pepper flavor.

Recipe
×

Chicken breast with button mushrooms

  • Slice the chicken breast and mix it with one tablespoon of light soy sauce, cooking wine, and starch, along with a little black pepper. Marinate for 20 minutes.
  • Sauté minced garlic until fragrant
  • Add the button mushrooms and stir-fry until soft.
  • Add the chicken breast, one tablespoon of oyster sauce and light soy sauce.
  • Sprinkle with black pepper and stir-fry evenly.

Crispy salt and pepper chicken wings corn

Super delicious crispy and salted chicken wings with corn!! The chicken wings are crispy on the outside and tender on the inside, fully flavored and dripping with sauce!

Recipe
×

Crispy salt and pepper chicken wings corn

  • Cut the chicken wings in half, add one spoonful of light soy sauce, oyster sauce, cooking wine, one spoonful of chili powder, salt and pepper, starch and one egg, and mix well. Marinate for 20 minutes
  • Mix one tablespoon of chili powder, cumin powder and one tablespoon of oil well
  • Cut the corn into pieces and sprinkle the sauce evenly over them.
  • Fry the chicken wings in hot oil until cooked.
  • Put in the corn chunks, fry them until they are slightly curved, and then serve them on a plate.

Honey Butter Crispy Chicken Cutlet

The crust is crispy, wrapped in honey frosting, and the meat is tender and juicy!

Recipe
×

Honey Butter Crispy Chicken Cutlet

  • Remove the bones from the chicken legs, add ginger slices, 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce, 2 tablespoons of light soy sauce, a little salt and black pepper, and marinate for 30 minutes.
  • Coat both sides of the marinated chicken leg fillets with starch.
  • Fry the chicken legs in hot oil until cooked.
  • Heat the pan again and add the butter.
  • Add minced garlic, one spoonful of light soy sauce and one spoonful of honey, and stir well.
  • Put the chicken leg fillets in, coat them with the sauce, and then take them out and cut them into pieces.

The preparation is simple. It's spicy and sour, and each bite is extremely satisfying.

Recipe
×

Lemon Spicy and Sour Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • Live shrimp, lemon, small chili peppers, minced garlic, coriander

Steps:

  • Sauce: Add minced garlic and dried chili peppers to a bowl, then add one tablespoon of oyster sauce, two tablespoons of light soy sauce, one tablespoon of rice vinegar, a little salt and a little cold boiled water, and stir well
  • Pour live shrimps, ginger slices and scallions into a dry pot, cover the pot over low heat and simmer for about 3 to 5 minutes
  • Put the peeled shrimps into the sauce, add lemon slices and coriander, mix well, and then marinate in the refrigerator for about 20 minutes

Buleberry Cheesecake

A simple and easy-to-make cheesecake with a refreshing flavor!

Recipe
×

Buleberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • Cheese: 220g, Whipping cream: 90g, Blueberry jam: 80g, Cornstarch: 10g, Eggs: 2, White sugar: 30g, Butter: 70g, Oreo cookie crumbs: 120g, Fresh blueberries: as needed.

Steps:

  • Crush the Oreo cookies without the filling and mix them with softened butter. Press the mixture onto the bottom and sides of a mold. Refrigerate until ready to use.
  • Soften the cream cheese and add white sugar, stirring until well combined.
  • Add the egg liquid in three portions, stirring well after each addition.
  • Add the heavy cream and cornstarch, stirring until smooth.
  • Mix in the blueberry jam and fresh blueberries.
  • Pour the cheese mixture into the mold and bake at 200 degrees Celsius for 20 minutes.
